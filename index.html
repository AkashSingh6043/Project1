Razer Blade 15 Gtx 1660

I baught the following product in july 2019, and I just have to say it's one of the best laptops I've ever spent my money on.

Over the past 3 years, I only had a problem with heating but that's a hardware issue with all ligh weight thin laptops, but other than this laptop should be in every gamer, or school student's back that's looking for a laptop with the following attributes listed below.

SPEED

This thin and light weight laptop can handle any 2020-21 game titles, and there's a good wifi card, and GPU that doesn't draw much power.

QUALITY

From the outside it looks like a expensive product, I feel like you're really paying for the design here and let's just say the design team really good job with this product.

KEYBOARD / TRACKPAD

This laptop has one of the best gaming keyboard that I've ever had. The trackpad doesn't feel cheap either it's fully responsive to window hand gestures.

SCREEN

When I first openned this laptop, I was mindblown how this laptop screeen is so thin, and it's bright I've never had to put the screen on full brightness.
